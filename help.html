How to Use the Planner

Quick screenshot tour

Follow the arrows. No stress.

A quick visual tour of the screens students actually use: set programme, plan semesters, enter grades, then check graduation readiness.

Do not skip this
Recommended plan is just a starter.

It only fills the schedule. Graduation readiness needs grades for every planned course.

Step 1

Pick the correct programme first

Use the top dropdowns for programme, specialization, English path, and language. If this part is wrong, the rest of the planner will be wrong too.

Start with these dropdowns
Step 2

Build the semester plan

Open Planner. Load the recommended structure if you want a fast start, then add or move courses until the semesters make sense.

Open Planner
Add courses here Move course by changing semester
Step 3

Enter grades course by course

After results are out, pick a grade inside each course card. Blank grade means incomplete, so CGPA and graduation status will stay blocked.

Grade dropdown lives here
Step 4

Use the Graduation Checklist

This is the reality-check screen. It tells you what is still missing: grades, credits, prerequisites, failed courses, and category requirements.

Open Graduation Checklist
Look at this status first No panic. Fix these one by one.
Step 5

Check GPA and credit load

CGPA Simulation shows semester GPA and credit load. If only some grades are entered, it shows progress instead of pretending the CGPA is final.

Open CGPA Simulation
Watch heavy semesters here
Step 6

Download a backup before you leave

The planner saves in this browser only. Before changing device or clearing browser data, open Backup & Reset and download a backup file.
